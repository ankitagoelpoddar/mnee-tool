Wallet Creation:
========================================
./mnee-man -wallets <x>  

where <x> is the number of wallets to create.


FOR FUNDING WALLETS AFTER CREATION
=========================================
./mnee-man -wif <x> -address <y> -mneeUrl <z> -fundAmount <a> -fund true 

where <x> is the sender wif with <y> the corresponding address related to the wif, <z> is the base-url for the env you are testing and <a> being the fund amount for each address in the wallets.

=========================================
./mnee-man -mneeUrl "https://stg-api-cosigner.mnee.net" -parallel 100 -partial true 

where <x> is the number of threads to use for creating hexes.
